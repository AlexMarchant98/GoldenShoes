Here you can find all the information you need to return an item to us; please follow the steps listed below.

Ensure you have the following:

  • The item(s) you wish to return
  • The reciept that came with the delivery
  • A return postage label

  1. Print out the returns form found here.
  2. Fill out all information on the returns form.
  3. Place the returns form and item(s) you wish to return in a secure package.
  4. Add the return postage label onto the package, ensuring the address is as follows:

    Golden Shoes, Building 10

    London Way

    AB12 3CD
  5. Deliver the package and await confirmation from us that we have recived the package.
  6. On reciept, we will inspect the items to ensure they are valid for a return/refund. In the case we are unable to do so, you will recieve an email with an explanation and the items will be returned to you. Otherwise, you will receive your return/refund.

Please allow up to 2 weeks for this process, if you have not heard anything by then, please contact the delivery service you used to send the package.
